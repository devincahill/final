Financing

For online purchases we gladly accept the following forms of payment; Visa, MasterCard, American Express, Discover PayPal

By law, we must collect sales tax for orders shipping to states where sale tax is applicable. Shipping fees may also be subject to tax in certain states.

Delivery

Durham House of Pets ships nation-wide, including Alaske, Hawaii and all U.S. Territories. Shipping costs are determined by the total weight of the order and by the shipping method selected.

    Continental United States

  1. Freight (8-12 business days)
  2. Standard (3 – 7 business days)
  3. 2nd Day (2 business days)
  4. Next Day (1 business day)

    Alaska, Hawaii & U.S. Territories

  1. Freight (2 - 4 weeks)
  2. Standard (1 – 2 weeks)
  3. Priority (3 – 6 business days)
  4. *Exact delivery date is subject to change depending on location

Returns

If you or your pet is not 100% satisfied with your purchase, you can return it to any of our stores in person our mail it back to us using the return address on the original package.
