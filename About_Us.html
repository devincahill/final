Company History

Durham House of Pets was founded by Phillip and Marry-Ann Smith in the quaint town of Durham New Hampshire in 1953. Durham House of Pets started as a small pet store for local pet owners, customers took great satisfaction in the high-quality pet friendly products and outstanding customer service. In the early days Durham House of Pets prepared its own pet food from fresh high-quality meats and local sourced vegetables; always focusing on pet health and happiness. Word quickly spread about “The Mom & Pop pet shop”TM, now Durham House of Pets is proud to be ranked amongst the largest pet suppliers in the nation.  With East and West coast offices, and an online retail market, Durham House of Pets has grown from a small local pet store into a national icon, without sacrificing that home town feel. 

Why Us?

Here at Durham House of Pets we pride ourselves on our top-quality products and outstanding customer service, our goal has always been your pet’s health and happiness. Our online retail store allows us to bring our small home town feel directly to you, our 24-hour customer service center is based in the United States and always ready to assist you. We only sell high-quality products that are made in the USA so your pets only receive the best.
